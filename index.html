Professional Identity

Consulting/MSP work gives you a unique advantage: you see what “good” looks like across many organizations—and you also see the recurring failure modes: messy permissions, unclear ownership, manual joiner/mover/leaver steps, underused tools, and undocumented tribal knowledge.

My value is bringing structure to those environments: define ownership, establish governance baselines, automate repetitive work, and document what matters. I’m now intentionally leaning into roles where I can apply that same approach with deeper ownership over a single company’s systems as they scale.

My Delivery Framework

Discover → Stabilize → Automate → Scale → Enable

A repeatable method I use across engagements to reduce risk, improve productivity, and create systems that are easier to run over time.

Discover

Inventory systems, owners, permissions, and workflows to surface friction, risk, and dependencies.

Stabilize

Implement governance baselines, access models, and documentation to restore operational clarity.

Automate

Remove manual work via scripting, integrations, and provisioning automation—especially for JML.

Scale

Standardize role-based access, reusable playbooks, and escalation/vendor workflows that scale.

Enable

Drive adoption with training, self-service patterns, and clear handoffs across teams.

Measure

Track time-to-provision, ticket categories, audit findings, and adoption signals to show progress.
